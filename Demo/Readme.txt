This is a video showing the gameplay of the game
This game has been developed in C++ with SFML.
It is a mini roguelike in which the player must reach the flag at the end of the ship.
Battles are turn based. The player can use a weapon, whose effects he must discover and memorize. With each defeated enemy, the player gets a random weapon from the enemy.
The starting weapons of the player and each opponent are drawn randomly for more fun!
